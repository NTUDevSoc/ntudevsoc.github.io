About

Hack-A-Duck is a hackathon ran between NTUs computer science department & DevSoc (a student society under the same department)

It is a 24hr overnight hackathon where you have a variety of briefs to pick from either university or a sponsor based and just have a fun time!

There is also a variety social opportunities between taking a break and playing some games or joining one of the talks or workshops

Food & drinks is also provided during the event


The best part! It's free to join
